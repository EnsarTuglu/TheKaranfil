In short, what is this game-like thing The first game-like thing in the 2D Platform Adventure genre that I developed for Failure Cave Jam 24, yes "game-like" because both AlesKranov and Failure miss a small detail that is necessary to be a game. "BOTH ARE NOT FUN" yes, they both try to tell a story and you walk (I don't know if you can believe it, but in Failure you can even shoot) and that's it. But Malfunction is the game that made me realise that the games I developed lacked fun.

What I Did?

  • I will never forget you for being the first game that put the fun factor in my head.
  • For the first time I wrote a Game Design Document for a game I developed myself.
  • Slowly I started to write simple codes (like walking) myself.
  • I learnt the Aseprite interface and created my first Pixel Art game drawings and animations
  • I added sound to my game for the first time. (I didn't even realise that it could be so important before adding sounds, but it was very good that I experienced it)
