What is this in a nutshell The gang called "Karanfil", which hunts illegally and cuts down trees, causes great damage to nature. Aleskranov, a friend of nature, loses both legs to one of the traps set up by this gang while travelling in the forest... Writhing in pain, he realises the magic book next to him Ales takes this book and gains marvellous features such as flying. With these powers, he sets out to avenge his lost legs and to defeat Karanfii, the head of this gang that causes great damage to nature. (Then I can swear that when I wrote this script, I thought it was a perfect and impressive story)
